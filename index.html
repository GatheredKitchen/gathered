Getting Started

Up and running
in minutes.

Three simple steps from zero to your entire recipe collection living in your pocket.

01 👤
Create Your Account

Free in seconds. Just your name, email, and a password — and your personal collection is ready to go.

Free plan includes 10 recipes. No credit card required to get started. Upgrade to Pro anytime for unlimited recipes, ebook generation, and more.
02 📷
Scan Your Recipes

Point your camera at any recipe — handwritten card, cookbook page, screenshot, or printed sheet. Gathered's AI reads it instantly.

Tips for best results: Good lighting, card facing up, full recipe visible in frame. Multi-photo scanning supported for long recipes across multiple cards.
03 📱
Add to Your Home Screen

Install Gathered like an app — right on your home screen, one tap away whenever you need it in the kitchen.

  1. Open usegathered.app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — Gathered appears on your home screen
  1. Open usegathered.app in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — Gathered appears on your home screen
What Gathered Does

Everything your
recipes deserve.

Feature 01 📷
AI Recipe Scanning

Point your camera at any recipe — handwritten cards, cookbooks, screenshots, printed sheets. Gathered reads it exactly as written, preserving your cook's personal notes and measurements.

Feature 02 ⚖️
Instant Recipe Scaling

Cooking for 2 or 12? Tap ½×, 1×, 2×, or 3× and every ingredient updates instantly. No math. No mistakes. No "wait, what's ¾ of ⅔ cup?"

Feature 03 🌾
Beautiful Share Cards

Share any recipe as a stunning branded card — full ingredients, instructions, your name on it. One tap. Beautiful every time. Spreads Gathered with every share.

Feature 04 📚
Your Personal Ebook

Generate a beautiful, personalized cookbook with your name on the cover, your recipes inside, organized by category. Export it, print it, or gift it. A cookbook of your recipes.

Feature 05 ✏️
Edit Everything

AI scans it, you perfect it. Edit the title, swap the category, fix an ingredient, add the note only your family would know. Every detail under your control.

Meal Planner + Smart Grocery List

Plan your week by dropping recipes onto any day. Gathered builds your grocery list automatically — ingredients combined, quantities scaled, grouped by aisle — then sends it straight to your phone. Get in early. 🌾
