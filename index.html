Getting Started

Up and running
in minutes.

Three simple steps from zero to your entire recipe collection living in your pocket.

01 👤
Create Your Account

Free in seconds. Just your name, email, and a password — and your personal collection is ready to go.

Free plan includes 10 recipes. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro anytime for unlimited recipes, ebook generation, and more.
02 📷
Scan Your Recipes

Point your camera at any recipe — handwritten card, cookbook page, screenshot, or printed sheet. Gathered's AI reads it instantly.

Tips for best results: Good lighting, card facing up, full recipe visible in frame. Multi-photo scanning supported for long recipes.
03 📱
Add to Your Home Screen

Install Gathered like an app — right on your home screen, one tap away whenever you need it in the kitchen.

  1. Open usegathered.app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — Gathered appears on your home screen
  1. Open usegathered.app in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add
